Gros Morne Justice: Chapter 5

11/Aug 2015
bears
Plot summary Jack keeps feeding the bears and is training them to respond to commands. ~*Winter passes*~ In the spring, the male bear has come out of hibernation, but Jack finds the lady bear dead from a vicious attack. Jack and Norm are becoming annoyed with one another. Assorted thoughts
  • Jack likes taking care of the orphaned bears because “it was nice to be needed”. Ummm...paralyzed wife might need you a bit, dude?
  • Bears have clearly defined gender roles!
  • Beat your kids! Beat your bears! It’s the only way for them to learn.
  • Jack still has occasional fits of rage
Updated Plot Predictions
  • All signs still point to Jack leading a man-eating bear army, and a showdown between Jack and Norm!
