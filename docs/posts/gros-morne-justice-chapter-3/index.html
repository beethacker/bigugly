Gros Morne Justice: Chapter 3

17/Jul 2015
bears
Plot Sumarry Chapter 3 basically deals with the court case against Jack, who is being held liable for Rick’s illegal killing of the bear cub. Jack hires a lawyer and has his day in court, but ultimately, the judge rules against him, stripping him of his outfitters license. Assorted thoughts
  • This chapter doesn’t have anything too crazy in it--it’s just there to move the plot along to where Jack is no longer an outfitter.
  • However, we do get this progressive gem: She must know her stuff, Jack thought, and she might be just the ticket for the prosecutors in Corner Brook. “I don’t have any problem with a woman as my attorney,” he said aloud. (To no one in particular.) Thanks for clearing that up, Jack!
Updated plot predictions
  • Nothing notable happened in this chapter that wasn’t outlined in the back cover summary, so all our current plot predictions will stay in place.
